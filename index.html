Peanut Butter & Jelly

    Ingredients:

  • Loaf of Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly/Grape Jelly
  • Spoon
  • Napkin

    Procedure:

  1. Peanut Butter of your choice
  2. A Jar of Jelly of Your choice strawberry, grape
  3. Grab a loaf of bread, grab three pieces out
  4. Grab a spoon and use the back of it
  5. To scoop the peanut butter and spread it on the bread
  6. Wipe off spoon onto napkin
  7. Use spoon to scoop out jelly and spread on bread
  8. Put the pieces of bread together
