Mexican Rice

    Ingredintes

  • Comet rice
  • Caldo de Tomate
  • Ground Cumin
  • Pepper
  • garlic powder

    Procdeure

  1. Two cups of comet rice
  2. Brown the rice using oil on hot pan
  3. Obtain 2 halves of onion and bell pepper
  4. Put 4 cups of water into the pan
  5. Time to season: 2 and a half scoops of Caldo de Tomate
  6. Get pinch of Ground cumin,pink salt, garlic powder,Pepper
  7. Let the rice simmer for 15 min
